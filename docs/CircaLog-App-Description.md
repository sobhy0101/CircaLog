# CircaLog — App Description

## Short Description (GitHub repository "About" field — 1 line)

Sleep tracking PWA for Non-24-Hour Sleep-Wake Disorder. Actogram
visualization, cycle-based logging, offline-first. Built with React + Vite +
Supabase.

---

## Medium Description (GitHub README intro / app stores)

CircaLog is an offline-first Progressive Web App built specifically for people
living with Non-24-Hour Sleep-Wake Disorder and other circadian rhythm
disorders. Every mainstream sleep app assumes a fixed 24-hour schedule —
CircaLog does not. It tracks the drift in your sleep cycle over time, displays
it as a clinical actogram, calculates your free-running period, and generates
reports you can hand directly to your doctor.

Log your sleep in under 10 seconds. Understand your pattern over months.
Finally have an app that was built for you.

## Long Description (Landing page / marketing copy)

### For people who live outside the clock

If you have Non-24-Hour Sleep-Wake Disorder, you already know the problem.
Your body clock doesn't tick at 24 hours — it drifts. Every day, your natural
sleep time shifts a little later. Within weeks, you've gone all the way around
the clock. Sleep Cycle can't show you that. Pillow can't show you that.
No mainstream sleep app was ever built to show you that.

CircaLog was.

**What makes CircaLog different:**

CircaLog's primary visualization is an actogram — the same tool sleep
researchers and neurologists used to study circadian rhythm disorders. Your
sleep is plotted as a band that slowly drifts diagonally across the chart over
weeks and months. At a glance, you can see exactly how your cycle is moving,
how fast it's drifting, and whether anything — medication, light exposure, life
events — has affected its course.

**Built around cycles, not calendar days.**

Every entry in CircaLog is assigned a cycle number alongside its calendar date.
Your condition doesn't care what day of the week it is. CircaLog doesn't either.

**Your data. Your device. Your choice.**

CircaLog works fully offline. Your sleep data lives on your device first, in
your browser's local storage. If you choose to sign in with Google, it syncs to
a secure cloud database — but that's always optional. You're never required to
create an account to use the app.

**Features:**
- One-tap sleep/wake timer or manual time entry
- Automatic nap detection (sessions under 3 hours)
- Actogram drift chart with [ 1W ] [ 1M ] [ 3M ] [ 6M ] [ 1Y ] [ All ] views
- Free-running period calculation (unlocked after 14 days of data)
- Weekly and monthly health summaries
- CSV and PDF export for doctors and specialists
- Dark mode by default
- Installable as a PWA on Android and desktop
- No ads. No subscriptions. Open source.

CircaLog is built by someone who lives with Non-24 — for everyone who does.
